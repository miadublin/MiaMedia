Work

Here are some of the things i can do for you: Create your Brand online visual identity • Social media Ads, covers and images • Banners • Flyers • Labels • Infographics • Logo Design • Icons • Brochures • Business card • Birthday card • Presentation • Branding & Brand Management • Wedding Invitation • Xmas card • Postcard • E-book and magazine covers • Photoshop images (advanced retouching and beautifying) • Remove background • Make pictures brighter/darker • Merge images

I will work directly with you at each stage of the creative process—it should never be: here are 3 "logos," pick one. Rather the design process is a series of successive approximations until we get the end result where you need it to be.
